About Us Unison

Unison is a highly robust consensus wallet which is controlled and funded solely by $UNISON holders providing revenue share for all. A percentage of tax revenue is sent directly to the wallet of the Telegram bot which holders can verify their wallet in to vote on tokens to buy. At the end of each cay, the profits made buy the bot (Current wallet balance - Daily taxes generated) will be withdrawn from the consensus wallet and distributed to top holders of the token, allowing users to earn passive income from the community trades.

How It Works

Each holder has a set amount of votes allowed per day, allowing them to choose which tokens they would like to buy and can vote on tokens requested by other users. After a token gets a certain number of votes, after a set period of time or both, a token will be purchased on Uniswap. The same process is done for selling. Unison's wallet is funded through both tax collection and community donations, allowing the wallet to get topped up each time a holder sells.
